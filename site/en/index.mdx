BC Mod Manager (BMM) lets you install, manage, and load mods for
[Bondage Club](https://www.bondageprojects.com/club_game/) from one simple
in-game panel. It works with the mods you already know (it's FUSAM-compatible)
and adds a clear view of what loaded, what failed, and what's out of date.

Once installed, a **BC Mod Manager** button appears on the login / preferences
screen — click it to open the manager.

Prefer a bookmarklet or the console instead? See
[getting-started](getting-started) for the other ways to run BMM and a short
walkthrough of your first install.

## What you can do

- **Install and manage mods** — search, install, remove, and switch between
  stable / beta / dev versions.
- **See what's happening** — a load window shows which mods loaded, which failed,
  and why.
- **Add your own sources** — point BMM at custom registries or direct script
  links.
- **Stay up to date** — BMM speeds up startup with caching and tells you when a
  mod has a newer version.
- **Works with existing addons** — compatible with FUSAM mods, so most things
  just work.
- **Your language** — available in English, 简体中文, 繁體中文, Deutsch, Français,
  Русский, and Українська.
